Exhibit Description

Jalisco Historia is an Onomy Labs Interactive Wall sliding display exhibit for Museo Trompo Magico in Zapopan, Jalisco, Mexico. The exhibit is a 3D interactive wall that allows visitors to explore the history of Jalisco, Mexico. The exhibit is designed to be a fun and engaging way for visitors to learn about the history of Jalisco. The exhibit features a variety of interactive elements, including touch screens, motion sensors, and sound effects. Visitors can explore the history of Jalisco through a series of interactive games and activities. The exhibit is designed to be accessible to visitors of all ages and abilities.

Software

The software comes in two parts, an Android App that provides a conduit between the position sensor and the other part, a ReactJS/NextJS webapp.

Hardware

The hardware consists of an extruded aluminum structure with steel bearing rails. A 55" Samsung display is mounted to a frame with rollers that allow it to move smoothly and easily along the rails. A Samsung Galaxy Pad S9 is attached to the back of the display along with a Phidgets High Speed encoder.
